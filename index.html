What's In A Detail?


A birthdate here. A family photo there. A board affiliation, an old username, a child’s school.

Individually, each data point might appear trivial or benign. However, danger hides in their aggregation.

Sophisticated adversaries don’t look at data in isolation. They work with what’s already visible, cross-referencing these seemingly disparate data points. When combined, they create a detailed, dynamic portrait of your lifestyle, financial standing, relationships and behavioural patterns.

This correlation effect makes seemingly benign information dangerous because it builds a contextual narrative about individuals and their family- one that can be inferred and weaponized by anyone with intent.

It’s about what can be inferred and predicted by anyone with intent.

These narratives don’t require breaches or malware. They begin with what’s already visible. When mapped correctly, a digital footprint becomes a manual for fraud, coercion, and surveillance.

For our clients, what’s in a detail is the margin between safety and exposure.
